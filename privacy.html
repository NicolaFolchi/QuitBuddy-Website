Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

QuitBuddy is built by Nicola Folchi. This policy explains how the macOS app and the QuitBuddy website handle personal information.

Data Collection

QuitBuddy does not collect, transmit, or store personal data. All settings, rules, and diagnostics remain on your Mac and never leave your device unless you explicitly export them.

Permissions

  • Accessibility is required to raise apps and send system-defined quit commands. No keystrokes or window contents are recorded or transmitted.
  • Input Monitoring lets QuitBuddy observe Cmd+Q so it can confirm quits. The app does not log, analyze, or upload keyboard input.

Both permissions are optional but necessary for the app’s core functionality. You can revoke them at any time in System Settings ▸ Privacy & Security.

Purchases

QuitBuddy uses Apple’s StoreKit and App Store Commerce for payments. All purchase data is handled by Apple; the developer does not receive credit card details or personal information beyond what Apple makes available to developers (aggregated sales/analytics).

Third-Party Services

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporters. The website is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages and may collect standard server logs.

Children

QuitBuddy is rated 4+ on the App Store and is suitable for all ages. The app does not collect personal information from any user, including children, and it contains no advertising or web browsing.

Contact
